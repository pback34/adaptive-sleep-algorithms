## Notable Wrist-Worn Pulse Oximeters

### Philips WristOx2

The Philips WristOx2 is an FDA-approved oximeter designed for continuous oxygen monitoring[3]. It offers:

- Long-lasting battery life
- Enhanced memory for extended data collection
- Bluetooth connectivity (in some models)

While primarily intended for professional healthcare settings, it's considered one of the best options for continuous monitoring.

### Wellue SleepU

The Wellue SleepU is a compact wrist-worn oxygen monitor specifically designed for overnight use[5]. Key features include:

- Continuous recording of SpO2, pulse rate, and body movements
- Vibration alerts for low oxygen levels
- Up to 16 hours of battery life
- Companion app for data analysis and reporting

### Checkme O2 Wrist Oxygen Monitor

This device offers comprehensive monitoring capabilities[6]:

- Continuous tracking of oxygen levels and pulse rates for up to 7 nights
- Smart alerts when oxygen levels drop below a preset threshold
- 72-hour battery life
- Data storage for up to 4 sessions (10 hours each)
- Companion app and PC software for detailed analysis
